About LMS

A Learning Management System for the students of SZIC to get to keep in touch with the teachers and remain updated about their studies without any obstacle.

Students and teachers need to remain updated about their syllabus and new study material on-time. With the delay, one ought to get in trouble. The application therefore, aims to bring students and teachers on one platform. With a simple registration from student, be it an alumni or a new student, they can get hands-on all the notes of all four years of Bachelors. Similarly, a teacher can upload an assignment for the students which they can submit on the same platform. A unique feature for the teachers is that they can take online exams with a limited time option, so the students can to complete it only within the given time-frame.
